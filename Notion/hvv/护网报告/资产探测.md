 利用 httpx -l 1.txt -silent -status-code -title -tech-detect -ip 获取资产信息
```获取对应的高价值站点
# API类
Select-String high_value.txt -Pattern "/api|/v1|/v2|/rest" | Out-File api.txt

# 后台/登录
Select-String high_value.txt -Pattern "admin|login|后台|管理" | Out-File admin.txt

# Swagger / 接口文档
Select-String high_value.txt -Pattern "swagger|api-docs" | Out-File swagger.txt

# Spring Actuator
Select-String high_value.txt -Pattern "actuator" | Out-File actuator.txt

# 可能绕过（401/403）
Select-String high_value.txt -Pattern "401|403" | Out-File bypass.txt
```
